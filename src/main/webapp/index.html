Login with Spotify to start pooling your group's playlists. Wayvepool will create a Spotify playlist with songs that you all have in common.
What is Wayvepool?
Its a tool that helps groups of people listen to music. It creates a single, accesible Spotify playlist that is customized to fit the needs of many people at once.

Here's how it works. Each group member logs into Spotify through the Wayvepool portal. The app scans each user's playlists and compares track data between users. The app finds what songs people have in common and organizes them into one master playlist that everyone will enjoy.
